Welcome to the Crystal Collector!

How to play:

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below, each with a different value. By clicking on a crystal, you will add its specific value to your total score.

The values of each crystal will stay the same throughout the course of the game, but you won't be able to see them. Also, each time you start a new game, these values will change.

Your goal is to add up enough numbers to reach the taret number. However, if you go over this number, you lose.

Are you up for the challenge? If so, click on a crystal to start playing!
